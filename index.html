The project

We, at Canto, are Setting up a battery cell production plant in Kenya with maximum annual production capacity of 1 GigaWatt-hour. Our focus is on clients in the automotive, residential energy storage, and battery energy storage systems.

The Team

The founding team has a combined 25 years of experience in lithium battery production, material science, engineering, and investment banking across Africa, Europe and the world. Across the team, we have lithium battery application and development expertise as well as four registered, and counting, globally recognized patent applications in lithium battery use.

Why Kenya?

Our first factory in Kenya is well suited to producing battery cells due to Kenya's highly renewable electricity generation that will minimize our own GHG footprint and those of the batteries we shall produce, her strategic location in East and Central Africa, and finally her Pro-investment policy through Special Economic Zones (SEZs) and preferential trade agreements. Registering our factory for the rules of origin shall further allow us to issue a Certificate of Origin (COO) for our export clients to leverage the incentives offered by various trade agreements across the globe, including EAC (East African Community), COMESA (Common Market for Eastern and Southern Africa), AfCFTA (African Continental Free Trade Area), EUR1 (for exports to European Union countries), and GSP (Generalized System of Preferences).

What We Shall Offer

Automotive

Battery cells to create vehicle battery packs for use and replacement of batteries used in hybrid and fully electric vehicles

Residential energy storage

Battery cells used in creation of battery packs for use in residential and solar home systems to store solar and grid energy.

Grid storage systems

Battery cells for use in large battery packs for grid stabilization and integration of power plants into the electricity grids.
